    Between us



    I was ice and he was fire,
    He was all that I desired.
    He was the rainbow when I was rain,
    He was the analgesic to all my pain.


    He could dig me to shallows,
    He could soar me to the sky.
    He would be my wallow,
    Or the reason why I cry.


    Sometimes, with the warmth of sunshine,
    But at times, burning like scorched heat.
    He was my addiction just like wine,
    He was what no one could beat.


    He was the core of my evolution,
    He was my perspicuous vision.
    He was my guided supposition,
    He was my only unassailable intuition.


    He could expose all my scars,
    And he could heal all my sore.
    Forever may I be his prisoner in bars,
    But I will always need him more.


    He is all the sins I commit,
    And he's my only virtue.
    In my life,his chaos I won't ever remit,
    If I am unconditionally true.


    Let me drown in his ocean,
    Or die starving in his desert.
    I will love him incessantly is my notion,
    That is all that I can assert.


    He was mine and I was his,
    Whatever may be the situation.
    All I needed ever was just this,
    He was my chaotic perfection.






    When I Found You

    There was dark, but then I found light,
    Everything was wrong, you made it right.
    The dull in me was painted in a thousand hue,
    That's what happened when I found you.


    I was lost, you became my only known way,
    In the gloom, you were my ray.
    I found my true version completely new,
    That's what happened when I found you.


    I happened to smile,
    Forget my agonies for a little while.
    My heart feels myself if I'm being true,
    That's what happened when I found you.


    I got scared of my memories,
    Which brought back the worries.
    Calm and peace replaced the inner ado,
    That's what happened when I found you.


    I was in pain but i healed because of you,
    I was at my worst but I am at my best because of you.
    I suppress my emotions but express them in front of you,
    I hated my fate but thank it now because I found you.
    It was hell and I found heaven with you,
    That's what happened when I found you.






    The Fake Shows

    Tapping to the sounds of tunes around me,
    People having good times was all I could see.
    Then apparently the music paused-and an awkward silence prevailed,
    There was nothing common to talk about and so the fake fun bailed.


    And the fainting music the rain gave,
    Made me want to be a little naive.
    Why can't I take pride in doing wrong?
    Why can't I stay silent and not sing along?


    Why can't people around me know that I'm miserable and weak?
    Why do they presume charm on my smile when it's actually bleak?
    Shall I always appear strong when all I can feel is a void?
    How long can I hold when my inner self is completely destroyed?


    Why can't these people know what I really feel?
    Why do they judge instead of helping me to heal?
    Why does discussing my failures become a talk show?
    Wish people could know what I wanted them to know.


    And so, the music played again,
    These thoughts drowned which they may never recall,
    And this realisation faded just like the music of the rain,
    Maybe this world enjoys fake shows after all.






    I Remember

    I remember the night when you and me became we,
    But it was the fate which I didn't foresee.
    I remember that moment which flushed my soul,
    Now it pierces my heart like a deep hole.


    I remember the night you made me mine,
    Each element around us, a beautiful chime.
    I remember each time you looked and smiled,
    Which now feels like a jinx realizing how exquisitely you lied.


    Then came the night you said it,
    The night you decided to end it.
    I remember the tears that rolled down,
    And remember your face with no frown.


    I remember your heart that went cold,
    The one which was once enfolded in gold.
    I remember the way you looked away,
    Breaking the promise that you'll always stay.


    I remember the sigh of relief you took,
    And all the good memories you refused to look.
    I remember how I pleaded you to stay,
    Denying that there can actually be a way.


    Each hurting memory made it hard to elope,
    Drowning me in a gloom with no ray of hope.
    You made me face the hardest times and the worst,
    The boon of my life which felt cursed.


    Now all I remember is,
    That I made myself strong,
    It wasn't me who was wrong.
    All that happened was a celestial spell,
    I can now have the love that I give myself.
    Grateful to you for letting me realize my worth,
    Letting me have what I truly deserve.






    The Phases Of Life

    Looking at the rain,
    Bearing all the pain,
    Raindrops falling like my tears,
    I was confronting my tears.


    Recalling that sunrise,
    The clear aesthetic skies,
    Who knew the clouds would take it all,
    Who knew this would be the way I'll fall.


    I try, I try and I try again,
    Who knew life would be such a constraint,
    I ignored darkness over the bright moon,
    Oblivion of my impending doom.


    I'm falling apart like the autumn leaves,
    Difficulties like the hustling winds,
    I doubt that I'll ever find peace,
    Waiting to see if spring ever begins.


    A snow Angel living it all,
    As I witness the falling snow,
    This is a faint memory I now recall,
    As I'm frozen in the devilry of life I didn't know.


    Bt the morning is a pursuit of the gloomy night,
    Maybe this darkness will be invaded by light,
    Maybe my life is like this unpredictable weather,
    Maybe I just need hope, faith and belief altogether.


    The darkness and light all have their roles,
    Devote it all to what you desire,
    Fall down, get up, every time just soar higher,
    It is life, it has phases, it is its beauty you need to admire.
